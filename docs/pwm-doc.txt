Micro Servo 9g Specs:
Brown - GND in
Orange - PWM Signal in
Red - 5V power in

Relevant Pins:
P9 1 - GND out
P9 8 - 5V power out
P9 14 - PWM 1 out

Breadboard Setup:
F28       P9 1
F22       P9 8
F14       P9 14

J14-J12   470ohm Resistor
F12-F10   470ohm Resistor

J28       Servo GND in
J22       Servo 5v power in
J10       Servo PWM Signal in
